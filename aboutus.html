About Yogatree

YogaTree is an eco-sustainable company that aims to sell dedicated sports clothing and equipment in respect of nature. Yoga is a millennial practice that focuses on physical and spiritual well-being. Nature plays a fundamental role in this activity, as it is essential to create an authentic bond and stay in close contact with it. For this reason, YogaTree has decided to create an initiative that provides the possibility of planting a plant with the purchase of one of our products. Our goal is to allow people to understand their lifestyle, understand where to improve and remember that change is a sprout to be cultivated with small daily gestures. It is not a simple plant, it is a choice of life, a way of being, just like yoga.
